Covid-19 Still a Menace:

An Insight on live data of UK

Arpan Sarkar
09-02-2021

Covid-19 is still threatening the normal life across the globe. The new mutant as Delta variant is the new threat. Efficacy of vaccines are now open beyond doubt. Watch the current state of affairs with live data from UK. From middle of March 2020, covid-19 cases started picking up and within a month reached a high of 5486 and then tapered down to below 10 in 2020 July 1st week.
Again the rise in new cases picked up to more than 33.5K by Nov 12 2020 to fall down again to 1430 by Nov beginning dame year. And menacingly, by the end of 1st week of January 2021, the cases reached the peak with more than 68K. The cases fell down to 1640 by 10th of April 21. Again a V shaped rise in cases to above 47.7K by July 21, then fell to 25K in 1st week of August to rise again to 34K by end of August. New fatal cases rose from 1 on 6th March 2020 to 1123 on 10th April 2020. New fatal case fell to 1 by start of August 1st week. Fatal cases rose to above 1450 by start of Feb 2021.On May 3 new death case dropped to 1 again rose very mildly and fell to 0 on 1st June 21. Figure started rising and by end week of August a high of 133 observed.
